Inclusive Education Through Hearing & Learning Support

HearSmart2Learn is dedicated to advancing inclusive education by addressing hearing and learning challenges that affect children’s academic, communication, and social development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between hearing health, children's mental health, learning support, and inclusive education within underserved and diverse communities. We use research-based and evidence-based approaches.

We work with schools, families, healthcare professionals, and communities to provide assessment, early intervention, family support, and awareness.

Why It Matters

Many children experience hearing and learning challenges that go unnoticed, affecting confidence, communication, academic performance, and long-term opportunities.

Early identification and timely support can transform a child’s educational journey. Every child deserves to participate, learn, and thrive in an inclusive environment.

What We Do

We address listening barriers through classroom acoustic assessments and hearing screenings. We provide teacher-led screening tools such as CHAPS, SIFTER, and SAEBRS.

We support learners through referrals, aural rehabilitation, counselling, and family support. We also advocate for inclusive education and raise awareness about hearing and learning challenges.
