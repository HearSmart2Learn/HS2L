HearSmart2Learn is dedicated to advancing inclusive education
by addressing hearing and learning challenges
that affect children’s academic and social development.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to bridge the gap between hearing health, children's mental health, learning support, and inclusive education within underserved communities through research-based and evidence-based approaches.

We work with schools, families, healthcare professionals, and communities to provide assessment, early intervention, support, and awareness.

WHY IT MATTERS

Many children experience hearing and learning challenges that go unnoticed, affecting confidence, communication, and academic performance.

Early identification and intervention can transform a child’s learning journey.

Every child deserves to learn and thrive in an inclusive environment.

WHAT WE DO

• Classroom acoustic assessments and hearing screenings
• Teacher screening tools (CHAPS, SIFTER, SAEBRS)
• Referrals and aural rehabilitation
• Counselling and family support
• Advocacy for inclusive education and hearing health awareness
